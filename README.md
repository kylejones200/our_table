  calories: 350
  protein_g: 12
  carbs_g: 45
  fat_g: 14
---

## Ingredients

- 1 lb pasta
- 2 cups tomato sauce
- 1 cup cheese

## Instructions

1. Boil water in a large pot
2. Add pasta and cook according to package directions
3. Drain and serve with sauce and cheese

Any additional notes or tips for the recipe.
